## What is Hookit?

Hookit is India's fastest way for creators, artists, bloggers, and small businesses to share links, blog articles, and product showcases — without creating an account, without passwords, and without waiting.

Website: https://hookit.online
Tagline: Create. Share. Get Discovered.

## How Hookit Works

1. Go to https://hookit.online/hook/new
2. Choose your hook type: Link, Blog, or Product Showcase
3. Add your title, description, image, and the URL you want to redirect to
4. Optionally add your name, email, and category for discovery
5. Get your unique hook link instantly — share it anywhere
6. Track views and clicks on your hook page

No signup. No login. No app download. Create and share in under 30 seconds.

## Hook Types

| Type | Best For | What Happens |
|------|----------|-------------|
| **Link** | Sharing any URL — articles, videos, portfolios, social profiles | Visitor clicks → redirect tracked → lands on your URL |
| **Blog** | Long-form writing, tutorials, stories, guides | Visitor reads on Hookit → clicks "Read Full Article" → redirect tracked → lands on your blog |
| **Product** | Showcasing physical or digital products | Visitor sees product details → clicks "View Product" → redirect tracked → lands on your store/website |

All hooks include: title, description, cover image, category, tags, view count, click count, and creator attribution.
